Who is Andrés León?

Hi, my name is Andrés Felipe León Oyola. I was born on June 2, 1997 in Bogotá, Colombia. My father is Alfredo León and my mother is Diana Oyola. Since my childhood I always practice soccer and my first school was Juanito Moreno Soccer School and then I started playing in Independiente Santa Fe.

In addition to training soccer, I also practice swimming for 2 years in which I learn different swimming styles.

My favorite food is pizza and steak with French fries. I have a french poodle dog named Amenis, he is 11 years old. I like to travel and know new places. One of the places that I like the most and that I have not had the chance to go to is Alberta, Canada. Finally, my hobbies are playing soccer, billiard and going to the gym.
