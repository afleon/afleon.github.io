Project Description

Show Tracker is a mobile application that helps independent musicians, individuals with public or private places and people who like live music. What this mobile application makes is to establish a means of communication between independent musicians and between the owners of the places to arrange live performances. This mobile application was created for PMC and it is still being developed.

Project Details

  • Independent musicians access to a database of the associated places and helps them customize their search based on location, name of the place they wish to play to, places similar artists have played, and clubs the musician has performed at before.
  • Measures popularity based on four metrics of its service: reach, influence, access, and recency. Artists are then ranked according to genre and location.
